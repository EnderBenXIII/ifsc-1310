Slay the Spire

What's Slay the Spire?

Slay the Spire is a roguelike card game developed by the studio Mega Crit. In the game, you play as one of four characters and attempt to reach the top of the spire, climbing floors full of enemies, traps, and surprises along the way.

You achieve this goal with a deck of cards that you can build upon and upgrade on your journey to the top of the Spire. Whilst all four charcters start out with very similar decks, the cards and upgrades that become available to you vary wildly from character to character, allowing for unique experiences each time you play.

Gameplay

Gameplay in Slay the Spire consists of a combination of roguelike aspects and deck building with cards. The player selects one of four characters, each with their own unique starter decks and relics, and proceeds into the Spire. Whilst climbing floors The Player may encounter combats, shops, events, traps, and other such things that may help or hurt them as they attempt to progress further up the Spire.

Development

Slay the Spire was developed by the studio Mega Crit. Based in Seattle, the team is led by developers Anthony Giovannetti and Casey Yano. The goal of the game was to combine the roguelike style of game with deck-building card game aspects from games such as Dominion.

Reception

Upon it's initial release, Slay the Spire failed to meet launch expectations. It was not until a Chinese streamer played the game in front of 1.5 million viewers did the game surge in popularity. By March 2019, reports showed that the game had sold over 1.5 million copies. In regards to how streamers interact with their audience while playing a game, Giovannetti said A streamer isn't going to be afraid to speak their minds or insult you - they probably don't even know you're there most of the time.
