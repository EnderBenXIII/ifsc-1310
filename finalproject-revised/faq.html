FAQ

There are few tournaments that happen regularly. There's Smash @ Bears on Wednesdays, the UARK eSports club hosts tournaments on Thursdays, the UALR eSports club hosts tournaments on Fridays, the UAFS eSports club hosts tournaments on Saturdays, and there's another tournament called Tech or Die that happens on Sundays. You can find out more by contacting us!

Typically, tournaments comprise of best of 3 matches until there are only 8 competitors left. Matches that happen in this Top 8 are all best of 5. Brackets are also usually double elimination, meaning you have to lose 2 matches before you are eliminated from the tournament.

Some tournaments are pay to enter, others are free to enter. Typically this is outline when you register for the tournament. Payout for the tournament is also usually outlined when you register, but typically it's top 3 payout.

Most tournaments have a page on start.gg where you can pre-register for an upcoming tournament. Tournament Organizers will typically post the link to the tournament's respective registration page in some kind of announcment. You can find out more by contacting us!

Just come to tournaments! If you participate and play, we consider you a part of the scene!

Background

The Arkansas Smash Bros. Ultimate Scene has been around since the game came out back in 2018, and has gone through multiple generations of players, with many people making a name for themselves in the scene.

Since the pandemic, the scene has slowly been on the decline, but hopefully the scene will be able to bring some new blood in that is willing to make this scene become great again.
